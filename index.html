Peter Geselowitz - Artist's Biography



Peter Geselowitz's artistic portfolio, preserved below from his personal image collection, extends from fine-art oil-paintings, through advertising illustration, to large-scale commercial murals. Included are also numerous works by Patrick Cordingley who was a life-long friend, co-founder of their creative agency 'Pat&Pete', and who is considered one of South Africa's top impressionistic painters.
